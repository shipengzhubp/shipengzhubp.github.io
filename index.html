Biography

My research interests mainly include machine learning and weakly supervised learning. Currently, I am an assistant researcher (至善博后) at the PALM Group , School of Computer Science and Engineering, Southeast University, China. I received the Ph.D. degree from Southeast University, China in 2025, advised by Prof. Hui Xue, M.Sc. degree from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, China in 2020, and B.Sc. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China in 2017.
